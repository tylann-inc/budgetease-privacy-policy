BudgetEase — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025
Information We Collect How We Use Information Third-Party Services Data & Security

BudgetEase ("the App") is developed by Tylann Inc. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

BudgetEase does not require account creation, does not collect personal information, and does not store data on our servers. All data you enter into the App is saved locally on your device only.

However, the App may collect or rely on limited information through the following:

(a) In‑App Purchases

We use third‑party payment processors (Google Play / Apple App Store). These services may collect information required to complete your purchase. We do not receive or store your payment details.

(b) Advertisements

BudgetEase displays ads served by third‑party advertising partners (for example, Google AdMob). These partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interactions
  • Analytics data

Data collected by ad partners is governed by their own privacy policies.

(c) Permissions

The App may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Local Storage Permission — Used only to export budget reports or documents. No data is uploaded or shared.
  • Notification Permission (Optional) — Requested only if you enable reminders or notifications. Used solely for sending local notifications.

The App does not access photos, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive device data.

2. How We Use Information

Any data stored or generated by the App is used only for:

  • Managing your budgets within the App
  • Exporting reports when you request it
  • Sending notifications if you enable them
  • Improving app performance and user experience through anonymous analytics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data.

3. Third‑Party Services

The App may use the following third‑party services:

  • Google Play / Apple App Store Billing
  • Ad networks (e.g., Google AdMob)
  • Crash reporting or analytics tools

These services have their own privacy policies — please review them before using the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

All budgeting data remains on your device unless you export or share it manually. We do not store, process, or transmit your data to any server. Standard platform security (iOS/Android) protects your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

BudgetEase is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes.
